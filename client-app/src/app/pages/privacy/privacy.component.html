Privacy & Security

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This page explains how your data is collected, stored, and used within our app.

 Data Collection

As part of the personal trainer-client relationship, we collect the following personal information:

Client ID: Automatically generated by the system. Name: Client’s full name. Date of Birth: Client's date of birth for age verification. Gender: Client’s gender (options: Female, Male, Unspecified). Fitness Program: The fitness program chosen by the client (options: Fat Loss, Senior Fitness, Muscle Gain, Pre/Postnatal Fitness, Contest Preparation, Overall Fitness). Joined Date: Date when the client started with the personal trainer. Ending Date: Date when the client’s fitness program ends. Special Health Notes: Any specific health information shared by the client (optional). Trainer ID: Automatically generated by the system.

All information is collected for the sole purpose of providing personal training services and is never shared without explicit consent from the client.

 Data Security

Your data is securely stored in our remote database. We adhere to strict security protocols to ensure your personal information remains safe:

Encryption: All sensitive data (such as passwords) is encrypted during transmission and storage. Access Control: Only authorized personnel (personal trainers) will have access to client data, and this access is protected by secure authentication methods. Regular Audits: We perform regular security audits to identify and address any vulnerabilities in our system. Data Backup: We maintain secure backups of your data to prevent loss in case of system failure. Data Use

The data you provide will be used for the following purposes:

Client Management: To maintain a record of your personal training sessions, including your progress, goals, and fitness program. Communication: To send you relevant updates about your program or any changes to the service. Legal Compliance: To ensure compliance with relevant laws and regulations, including privacy and data protection laws.

We will not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with authorized service providers or contractors, but only when necessary to provide the services you request, and in full compliance with the Australian Privacy Principles.

 Data Storage and Retention

Your data is stored in a secure, cloud-based database. We retain your information for as long as it is necessary to provide our services. If you choose to terminate your relationship with the personal trainer, your data will be retained for a period of time in accordance with legal obligations and best practices. After this period, your data will be securely deleted.

 Your Rights

Under the Australian Privacy Principles, you have the right to:

Access Your Data: You may request access to the personal information we hold about you at any time. Correction of Data: If any of your personal information is incorrect or incomplete, you have the right to request corrections. Deletion of Data: You may request the deletion of your personal information, subject to certain legal and contractual obligations. Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw consent for us to use your data at any time. However, this may affect our ability to provide the services you have requested.

If you have any concerns about how your personal information is being handled, please contact us directly.
